Maritime Programs

Launch your career at sea with our specialized Tech Pro Track — Career Exit Pathway designed for the maritime industry.

Marine Engineering

at the Support Level (MESL)

Master the fundamentals of ship machinery, propulsion systems, and marine engineering operations. Prepare for a career as a marine engineering support officer.

  • Engine Room Operations
  • Mechanical Systems
  • Safety Protocols
  • Technical Diagnostics

Marine Transportation

at the Support Level

Develop navigation skills, seamanship, and deck operations expertise. Train to become a competent maritime deck support professional.

  • Navigation & Seamanship
  • Cargo Handling
  • Bridge Operations
  • Maritime Regulations

Why Choose Maritime?

A World of Opportunity Awaits

The maritime industry is the backbone of global trade, offering lucrative careers, international travel, and unmatched job security. At Rosemont Hills Montessori College, we prepare you for success on the open seas.

  • Global Career

    Work anywhere in the world with internationally recognized certifications

  • High Earning Potential

    Maritime professionals are among the highest-paid technical workers globally

  • Job Security

    90% of world trade moves by sea — demand for skilled mariners is constant

  • Pathway to Officer

    Support level is your stepping stone to becoming a licensed marine officer
